Pad Thai

Description

A less quick recipe, but let me try to make it easier for you.Heavily inspired by Hot Thai Kitchen.

Serves 4.

Ingredients

  • 14 oz of rice noodles
  • 1-2 lbs of protein of your choice
  • 4 oz tofu
  • 2 tbsp of neutral oil
  • vegetables:
    • 1 red onion or 1/4 cup chopped shallot
    • 2 tbsp pickled radish (use less if yours is strong)
    • 1 bunch of green onions
    • 3 cloves garlic
    • 2 cups bean sprouts
  • For Sauce:
    • 3-4 tbsp of palm sugar
    • 3 tbsp water
    • 3-5 tbsp tamarind paste
    • 2 tbsp fish sauce (sub with soy sauce)

Steps

  1. Soak the rice noodles in warm water for at least an hour.
  2. While the noodles soak, prep all the vegetables by washing, slicing and/or dicing them. Mince the shallots and garlic.
  3. Prepare the sauce! Start by melting the palm sugar in a bowl (microwave or stove). Then, add in the tamarind paste, water and fish sauce. Adjust to taste.
  4. Throw the oil into a pan and start cooking the protein until mostly cooked through (about 80%).
  5. Add in the tofu, shallots/onions and garlic and any other vegetables and continue stir frying until tender (about 3 min).
  6. Add in noodles and sauce and mix and stir fry for another 5 min. Add sauce if it starts to be too dry (not too much though!). Until it looks right. You can add in chili flakes if you like.
  7. Serve it, be as fancy or unfancy as you need. Enjoy!
