Yaki Udon

Description

Another quick recipe (notice a trend?). Yummy and customizable. Heavily inspired by Drive Me Hungry.

Serves 2.

Ingredients

  • 2 packs of udon noodles (preferably frozen)
  • 1-2 lbs of protein of your choice
  • 2 tbsp of neutral oil
  • vegetables of your choice! some suggestions:
    • 1 onion
    • 1 bunch of bok choy
    • 1 carrot
    • 8 oz of mushrooms
    • sweet or bell peppers
  • For Sauce:
    • 3-4 tbsp of brown sugar
    • 5 tbsp soy sauce
    • 2 tbsp of mirin
    • 2 tsp of rice vinegar
    • 4 tbsp oyster sauce

Steps

  1. Start boiling the water for the noodles. I recommend cooking them for 1-2min. Otherwise, cook the noodles according to package instructions. Optional: I like to rinse the noodles in cold water or trhow them in an ice bath immediately after straining from the hot water. This gives them a chewier texture. If you're leaving them to sit for a bit, sprinkle a little netural oil and mix to prevent sticking.
  2. While you wait for the water to boil, prep all the vegetables by washing, slicing and/or dicing them.
  3. Throw the oil into a pan and start cooking the protein until mostly cooked through (about 80%).
  4. Add in the onions and any other vegetables and continue stir frying until tender (about 3 min).
  5. Add in noodles and sauce and mix and stir fry for another 2-3 min. Until it looks right.
  6. Serve it, be as fancy or unfancy as you need. Enjoy!
