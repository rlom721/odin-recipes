"Peanut" Gochujang Noodles

Description

This is a quick and easy recipe for when I'm in the rush but craving something savory. Which is always. I can't have peanut butter so I sub with sunflower butter, but if you lack peanut allergies unlike me feel free to use PB.

Makes one serving.

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp sunflower butter
  • 1-2 tbsp gochujang
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp rice vinegar
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tsp of sesame oil
  • 6 oz of noodles of your choice
    • sub with garlic powder

Steps

  1. Cook your noodles per package instructions. Start boiling the water now if needed, so while you wait you can go on to step 2.
  2. In a bowl (how about the one you'll eat out of - if you're lazy about dishes such as yours truly) mix the rest of the ingredients. The non-noodle ones. Add and subtract according to taste.
  3. Once your noodles are cooked, here's a pro tip! Rinse them with cold water or throw in some ice cubes immediately after straining them from the hot water. This gives them a nice chewy texture.
  4. Throw them into the bowl with your sauce mix. Optional: Garnish them with chopped green onions, furikake, cucumber slices and maybe some kimchi. Up to you.
  5. Enjoy!
